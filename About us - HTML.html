Mission

The Friends of Cherry Valley National Wildlife Refuge are dedicated to the conservation and enhancement of the natural and cultural resources, rural character, and scenic beauty of the Cherry Valley Area.

Our History & Our Goals

Established in 2002, we are a 501(c)3 non-profit organization formed by concerned residents and conservationists. Since our establishment, we strive to meet the following goals:

  • Improve connections and communications with homeowners, landowners, and the community regarding conservation and land preservation.
  • Protect open space and environmentally sensitive areas.
  • Strengthen our Board to prepare future leaders.
  • Provide support to and community involvement in the Cherry Valley National Wildlife Refuge.
